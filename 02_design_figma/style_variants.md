# Style Variants

## Variant A — Madness Meets Wood

Primary direction.

### Feeling
Dark, warm, rustic, alive, slightly chaotic, handmade, weird in a good way.

### UI traits
- Charcoal background
- Warm cream text
- Wood-brown cards
- Terracotta/orange accents
- Rough sign-inspired typography
- Masonry image grid
- Big emotional shop photos
- Organic dividers and uneven labels

### Why it fits
The real shop is visually dense, warm, strange, handmade, and memorable. This direction translates the physical experience into a digital workshop.

---

## Variant B — Warm Cretan Artisan

Secondary comparison direction.

### Feeling
Cleaner, calmer, more premium, tourist-friendly, less chaotic.

### UI traits
- Warm cream background
- Dark brown text
- Clean product cards
- Softer handmade gallery feel
- More traditional artisan layout

### Why it exists
Useful as a safer fallback and to prove design exploration. Less accurate to Manolis than Variant A, but easier for a conservative client/team to understand.
