# Decision Log

Permanent record of major project decisions.

## 2026-06-08 — MVP strategy locked

### Decision
Build an inquiry-based catalogue first, not a full checkout webshop.

### Reason
The client has no structured inventory, no fixed prices, no payment setup, and unclear shipping rules. The assignment MVP allows a product gallery without prices and contact-based inquiry.

### Impact
Friday prototype will focus on gallery, product detail, WhatsApp/email inquiry, and contact/visit flow.

---

## 2026-06-08 — Main concept locked

### Decision
Use **When Madness Met Wood** as the main concept and **Don’t buy souvenirs. Buy originals.** as the supporting brand line.

### Reason
The shop images and source material show a visually dense, strange, handmade, warm, and memorable workshop. The website should translate that feeling instead of becoming a sterile ecommerce template.

### Impact
Design direction uses dark workshop background, warm wood colors, rough typography, and controlled chaos.

---

## 2026-06-08 — Prototype stack locked

### Decision
Use static HTML/CSS/JS with JSON product data for the two-week prototype.

### Reason
Fastest to build, easiest to explain, no backend/payment complexity, good for vibecoding.

### Impact
No React/Next/backend unless the project scope changes later.
