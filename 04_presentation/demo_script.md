# Demo Script

## Opening
This project is not designed as a generic souvenir webshop. The research and source material show that Manolis' strongest asset is the physical workshop experience: chaotic, handmade, funny, warm, and memorable.

## Demo flow

1. Show homepage hero
2. Explain slogan: “When Madness Met Wood”
3. Show “Don’t buy souvenirs. Buy originals.” as brand pillar
4. Open Originals / Gallery
5. Click or explain inquiry product flow
6. Show Visit / Ask page
7. Explain why checkout is postponed
8. Show roadmap to future ecommerce

## Key sentence
The correct first MVP is an inquiry-based catalogue, because the client does not yet have fixed inventory, fixed pricing, payment setup, or shipping rules.
