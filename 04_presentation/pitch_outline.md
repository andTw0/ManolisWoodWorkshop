# 15-Minute Presentation Outline

## 1. Ausgangslage
Manolis Tsouris is a Cretan olive wood artist with a memorable physical shop, strong tourist appeal, Facebook content, and an existing domain, but no functional website or webshop.

## 2. Problem
Tourists want to find him again and ask about international orders after leaving Crete, but there is no structured online presence, no organized product inventory, and no online payment setup.

## 3. Research insight
The main value is not only the products. Visitors remember the personality, the workshop, the humor, the chaos, and the feeling of discovering something original.

## 4. Target audience
International tourists who visited the shop, tourists planning a Crete trip, and handmade craft/gift buyers.

## 5. Concept
**When Madness Met Wood**  
**Don’t buy souvenirs. Buy originals.**

## 6. MVP
Homepage, gallery/catalogue, about/story, visit/contact, WhatsApp/email inquiry, EN/DE language structure.

## 7. Design direction
Dark, warm, rustic, handmade, lively, slightly chaotic.

## 8. Prototype
Static HTML/CSS/JS prototype with JSON product data and inquiry-based product flow.

## 9. Roadmap
- Stage 1: website/gallery/contact
- Stage 2: inquiry catalogue
- Stage 3: CMS/webshop
- Stage 4: checkout/payment/shipping
